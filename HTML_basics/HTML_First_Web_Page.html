What mexico is known for

Mexico is a known for it's rich culture, vibrant cuisine, stunning natural landscapes, and historical significance. When you visit mexico you can find real Mexican food with natural ingredents. You can see the culture that mexico has. Mexico's historical significance is the Chichén Itzá, Uxmal, Tulum, and Aztecs Teotihuacan. Mexico was also the birth place of choclate with the Aztecs and Mayans cultivating cacao plants. Mexico has Dia de los Muertos (Day of the Dead) that is vibrant it is a celebration of the life remembrance of loved ones who have passed away.
