Porsche 911

The Porsche 911 was first introduced in 1963. At the Frankfurt Interational Motor Show as the 901. Production on the 901 started in 1964. They leter changed the name from 901 to 911. The 911 is a modern car fits only two people. It doesn't look like a sports car at all. The 911 is a sports car and its top speed is 182 mph. It goes from 0-60 is 3.9 seconds or 2.6 if you have a different model of the car. The porsche 911 cost around $120,100 for a new one. If you were to get a older model of the 911 it would cost $62.500-$175,000.
